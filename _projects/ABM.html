## 1. Overview **AVSim** is a high-resolution **agent-based modeling (ABM)** framework designed to simulate the spread of **airborne** and **vector-borne** diseases by integrating **real-world GPS mobility data**, **behavioural clustering**, and **transportation models**. Unlike traditional compartment models, AVSim captures: - Individual-level mobility and behaviours - Social mixing patterns across occupations - Transportation-driven exposure - Vector ecology (for dengue) - Policy interventions and environmental diversity The system enables detailed epidemiological analysis and policy evaluation under realistic behavioural dynamics. --- ## 2.
